The Sword, The Spear, and The Bow

Wielder of the sword is very powerful in close combat. Only problem treats the situation like a game and doesnt realize the consequences of his actions.

Itsuki Kawasumi: The Bow Hero

The Bow hero excels at range attacks. Tries to act as a vigilante but is too focused on fame and reward.

Motoyasu Kitamura: The Spear Hero

The worst hero out of all three. This man will have you cringing throught the whole series. No Doubt he is very strong but also highly annoying.
